About Medlink Clinic

Medlink is a 100% black-owned and managed Human Consulting Company based in Pretoria, South Africa. Our business activities are focused on Human Resources Consulting, Medico Legal services, Psychometric Assessments, Career Guidance and are not restricted in nature.

We are a young company looking to push the boundaries of excellence and committed to delivering exceptional service to its clients. Our clients experience special treatment from our team due to our company being small medium enterprise.

Our Services

Our medico-legal experts provide professional services tailored to the needs of legal and corporate clients:

  • Assessment Interviews – in-depth evaluations of patients or employees
  • Workplace Collateral – reporting on occupational incidents or health concerns
  • Joint Minutes – collaborative reporting with multiple experts
  • Pre-trial Meetings – preparation and discussion of expert evidence
  • Court Attendance – providing testimony as expert witnesses

All reports and evaluations are conducted in compliance with professional standards and legal guidelines, ensuring accuracy, credibility, and timeliness.

Whether you are a lawyer, insurance professional, organization, or individual, Medlink Clinic's Medico-Legal experts deliver reliable, objective, and actionable insights to support your case or decision-making process.
